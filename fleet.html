Aircraft Categories

From nimble light jets to spacious heavy jets, our diverse fleet ensures the perfect aircraft for every mission.

Light Jets

Perfect for short to medium-range flights with exceptional fuel efficiency and agility. Ideal for business trips and quick getaways.

  • Passengers: 4-8
  • Range: 1,200-2,400 miles
  • Speed: 400-500 mph
  • Examples: Citation CJ3+, Phenom 300

Mid-Size Jets

The perfect balance of comfort, range, and performance. Spacious cabins with premium amenities for longer journeys.

  • Passengers: 6-9
  • Range: 2,000-3,500 miles
  • Speed: 450-550 mph
  • Examples: Citation Latitude, Hawker 900XP

Heavy Jets

Ultimate luxury for transcontinental and international flights. Spacious cabins with full amenities and extended range capabilities.

  • Passengers: 8-16
  • Range: 3,500-7,000 miles
  • Speed: 500-600 mph
  • Examples: Gulfstream G650, Falcon 7X

Ultra-Long Range

The pinnacle of private aviation. Fly anywhere in the world non-stop with uncompromising luxury and comfort.

  • Passengers: 10-19
  • Range: 6,000+ miles
  • Speed: 550-650 mph
  • Examples: Global 7500, Gulfstream G700

Helicopters

Versatile rotorcraft for short-distance travel, city transfers, and accessing remote locations with precision landing capabilities.

  • Passengers: 4-8
  • Range: 300-600 miles
  • Speed: 150-200 mph
  • Examples: AW139, Bell 429

Turboprops

Efficient and reliable aircraft perfect for short runways and regional travel with excellent fuel economy.

  • Passengers: 6-12
  • Range: 800-1,500 miles
  • Speed: 300-400 mph
  • Examples: King Air 350, TBM 940
